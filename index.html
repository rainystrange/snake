Rules

Use arrow keys to change snake's direction.

In short: Get all the colors except black.

Details:

Snake is green. Food is red. Eat the food. But don't eat yourself or go off the board!

Every 5th piece of food you eat levels you up! Leveling up gives you more points for each piece of food - but you also go faster, so watch out!

Water is blue. Drink the water! It gives you double points. You only get water after you level up a couple times - but hurry, it dries up after just a few seconds.

If you're extra special you'll get GOLD. Get that gold! It's worth TRIPLE points and slows you down a little, so it's very valuable - but it disappears fast.

Shrink Snake: Get four water or gold squares in a row (don't miss one!) and your snake will get shorter! A shorter snake will make it easier to navigate without eating yourself.

Bombs are black. Beware the bombs! Eat one and you explode. Poor snake.

Names and scores are only stored on your browser and will erase when you close the page. Don't worry, if your kids are playing no one else will see their names.
